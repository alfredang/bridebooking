Our Story

A studio devoted to the art of remembering.

Aurelia Bridal was founded on a quiet belief — that a wedding is not a single day, but a thousand small moments stitched together by light. For over a decade, we've followed brides through silk-laced mornings, garden ceremonies, and the slow, glittering hours of dusk.

Our work lives somewhere between fine art and fine memory: composed, unhurried, and deeply human. Whether you dream of a heritage church ceremony or a private elopement on a cliff at golden hour, we'll meet you there with film, with care, and with patience.

— Aurelia & the studio team
